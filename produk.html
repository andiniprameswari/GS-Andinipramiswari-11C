A Good Girl's Guide to Murder

Rp 163.000

Five years after schoolgirl Andie Bell was murdered, the case is considered solved when her boyfriend, Sal Singh, supposedly confessed and took his own life. But Pippa Fitz-Amobi, a determined and curious student living in the same town, believes the story doesn’t add up. For her final-year project, Pip decides to re-investigate the case, interviewing witnesses and uncovering details that were ignored or hidden. As Pip digs deeper, she begins to expose dark secrets buried beneath the town’s perfect image—and realizes that someone is desperate to keep the truth buried. What started as a school assignment soon becomes a dangerous pursuit of justice, forcing Pip to question who she can trust and how far she is willing to go to uncover the truth.

Good Girl Bad Blood

Rp 163.000

After solving the Andie Bell case, Pippa Fitz-Amobi is determined to leave investigations behind. But when her friend Connor’s brother, Jamie Reynolds, goes missing—and the police refuse to take the case seriously—Pip is pulled back into the world she promised to abandon. This time, Pip investigates publicly through a true-crime podcast, uncovering lies, hidden motives, and unsettling connections that suggest Jamie didn’t just disappear. As the case grows darker and more personal, Pip faces mounting pressure, danger, and the consequences of exposing the truth in the spotlight—learning that justice often comes at a heavy cost.

As Good As Dead

Rp 163.000

Pippa Fitz-Amobi is done with investigations—or at least, she wants to be. Traumatized by her past cases, Pip tries to live a normal life until she begins receiving anonymous threats from someone who claims she is responsible for their actions. When it becomes clear that the danger is real, Pip realizes she may be facing a killer who knows her too well. As fear takes over and the lines between right and wrong blur, Pip is forced into a final, haunting confrontation with the consequences of her search for truth. Dark, intense, and unsettling, As Good As Dead explores guilt, justice, and how far someone can be pushed before they break.

As Good As Dead

Rp 163.000

Five Survive tells the story of six friends on a road trip that turns deadly when their RV breaks down in a remote area with no phone signal. Soon, they realize they’re being deliberately targeted by a hidden sniper who demands the truth from one of them. Forced to stay awake for eight terrifying hours, secrets unravel, trust collapses, and the group must decide how far they’ll go to survive until dawn.
